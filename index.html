About This Project

Built by Apex students, for our neighbors

The Apex Community Hub was created by six students from Apex High School for the 2026 Technology Student Association Webmaster competition. We wanted to build something that actually helps people in our community—not just a demo site.

We researched real local organizations, verified contact information, and designed the site to be accessible for everyone: students, families, new residents, and seniors alike.
