📋 Submission Guidelines

  • ✅ Resources must serve Apex, NC or Wake County residents
  • ✅ Must be a legitimate nonprofit, government, or community organization
  • ✅ Free or low-cost services are prioritized
  • ✅ We verify all submissions before publishing
  • ❌ Commercial businesses are not eligible
  • ❌ Political or religious-advocacy organizations are not eligible

📬 What Happens Next?

  1. Our team reviews your submission within 3–5 business days
  2. We verify the organization's contact information and services
  3. If approved, the resource is added to the directory
  4. We'll email you to confirm when your submission goes live
